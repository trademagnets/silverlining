Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What should I expect on my first visit?
Your first visit includes a comprehensive exam, digital X‑rays, and a conversation about your goals. We’ll create a personalized treatment plan.
Do you accept dental insurance?
Yes, we work with most major PPO plans and will help you maximize your benefits. Contact us to verify yours.
What if I don’t have insurance?
We offer flexible payment plans, CareCredit, and an in-house membership plan for uninsured patients. Ask us for details.
Do you see children?
Absolutely! We’re a family-friendly practice and welcome kids of all ages. Our team makes dental visits fun and stress‑free.
What languages do you speak?
We speak English, Hindi, and Dogri. Spanish is also spoken by some staff members.
How do I schedule an appointment?
You can book online using the “Find Available Appointments” button, call us, or use the WhatsApp button.
